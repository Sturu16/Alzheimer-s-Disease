Georgian Alzheimer's Association

The mission of the Association: Georgia without Alzheimer disease, all citizens of Georgia are informed about Alzheimer.

" a) timely detection and support to the patients with Alzheimer disease.
b) raising public awareness to prevent the disease.
c) formation of regional infrastructure to support the programs under the auspices of the Association.
d) active participation in medical studies.
f) organizing and conducting international meetings, congresses, lectures and exhibitions and participation therein (in and outside of Georgia).
g) establishing contacts with governmental and non-governmental organizations of Georgia and foreign countries in order to achieve the goals set of the Association;
h) carrying out any other activity pursuant to the applicable laws of Georgia that shall facilitate the implementation of the goals and objectives of the Association and doesn’t contradict the legislation of Georgia"

Fund Taoba

"A person at any age has the right of decent life. Every society is under obligation to elderly people. It is its duty to create social harmony for them, which means on the one hand, guarantee to meet necessary social demands. On the other hand, it underlies the utilization of their skills, abilities and especially their experience in social life. At the same time, with respect to every elderly people, the society is limited in time by the nature. Due to this circumstance, it does not have right of postponement or mistakes, because it will not have a second chance. Therefore, the society should start ensuring dignified old age immediately and properly. All efforts taken for elderly is a human obligation and, besides, an investment in the future – we will be treated in our old age as we treat the elderly now."
